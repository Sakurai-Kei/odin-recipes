Medium rare steak

A delicious looking steak

A medium rare steak is very simple to make and it tastes great!. It can be enjoy by using any grade of meat, although more expensive choice tend to tastes better!

Before we get to cooking it, we need to get the ingredients and do some pep work on it

Ingredients required

  • 200g of beef meat or any suitable meat of your choice
  • Salt and pepper or any suitable all-purpose spice that you prefer
  • Cooking oil and butter

Steps

  1. Make sure the is not frozen. If it is, let it thaw slowly in the refrigerator for 24 hours
  2. Season the meat according to your taste
  3. Put some cooking oil(not too much) and ensure the pan is very hot
  4. Start cooking the meat. Ensure that you do not put pressure on it to avoid losing moisture
  5. Flip it after 30 seconds. Repeat this for a total of 2 minutes
  6. Take it out of the pan and let it rest for a while before enjoying it
