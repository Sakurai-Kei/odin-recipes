Sushi

An easy to make sushi

Sushi is a very large category of food. For this particular example, we are just using rice and egg to demonstrate how easy it is to make one!

Before we get to cooking it, we need to get the ingredients and do some pep work on it

Ingredients required

  • Cooked rice
  • Omelette
  • Japanese mayonaise or any mayonaise of your choice
  • Seaweed rolls

Steps

  1. Ensure that you have a cooked rice beforehand
  2. Make an omelette
  3. Put a seaweed roll on a flat cutting board
  4. Put rice, omelette and mayonaise and roll them into the seaweed
  5. Done! Quick sushi to enjoy
